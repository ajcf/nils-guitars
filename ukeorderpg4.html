Terms and Conditions

By clicking submit, your are requesting to receive an official quote for a build. By submitting this information, you are not ordering a ukulele, but simply inquiring about placing an order and giving me the information to come up with what will work best for you. Upon receiving your inquiry, I will review what you are interested in having built and contact you by telephone to discuss your selected options, make recommendations and answer any questions you might have. The price listed above is simply an estimate at this point, and may be different than what your final quote is. If you choose to have any special features added on, the price may be different than what is listed above.

After discussing the build with you, I will make you a final quote. With the final quote, I will include the information about the instrument (design, materials, features, etc.), warranty information and the estimated delivery date. In order to start your build, I will require 50% of the final price upfront, with the rest due upon completion.

All guitars will be sold with a 7 day return period, in which you will be able to return the instrument for 100% of its value, should it not meet your expectations.
