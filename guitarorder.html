Order a Guitar!

There are two ways to order a guitar from me. If you know roughly what you would like your guitar to be like (or even if only slightly), the best place is to begin is right here with this order inquiry form. Start by choosing a body style and the woods you think you would like and then answer the questions on the following pages. After submitting, I will go over what you have provided and get back to you to discuss your build. I will then finalize your order and send you a quote, which you can send back to me when everything seems they way you want it. Of course, you can always contact me directly.
