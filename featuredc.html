Featured Guitar : Red Maple Grand Concert

  • Top: Sitka Spruce
  • Back/Sides: Red Maple
  • Neck: 2 Piece Red Maple
  • Scale length: 25.4"
  • Fingerboard/Bridge: Ebony
  • Binding: Cream Ivoroid and Herringbone
  • Rosette: Herringbone
  • Finish: Hand-rubbed Cherry Burst/Nitrocellulose Lacquer

Maple makes excellent sounding guitars that have a unique and powerful sound. Because maple is so heavy, stiff and dense, light bracing and construction is particularly key to good tone. Assuming the instrument has been made lightly, maple has a voice with a serious growl to it. The sound is focused and bright with a distinctive, rolling low end. The best part about a maple guitar though, in my opinion, is the way it opens up. As you're playing softly and then give a firm strum, suddenly a whole new part of the instrument comes to life. With this comes powerful low end and bite that isn't there with a gentle touch. I recommend maple to players who want a guitar that strums particularly well and has a strong voice, clear focus and presence.
