Frequently Asked Questions

Do you do repairs?
Not at the moment. I recommend Owen Davidson at Downtown Sounds in Northampton.

Do you build electric guitars or basses?
Let me know what you're interested in and I might be willing to make it for you, but most likely not. If you have a specific need as a musician that I can fulfull, I'd love to help you out, but other than that, I've found that my creativity just isn't leading me in that direction

Are your guitars "custom" built?
Yes, I consider them all to be custom. Although I use the words "standard" and "model", each one is different and I always voice them uniquely. "Model" simply means that the instrument's body will be a certain size, and "standard options" refers to what features the instrument has.

Will you build me a jumbo or dreadnaught sized guitar?
Yes, I will. At the moment, it's not a standard guitar I offer, but eventually I would like it to be.

Can you build me an electric/acoustic guitar, mandolin, or ukulele?
Yes, I love building instruments for people to play out.

What kind of wood sounds the best?!
This question doesn't really have a definitive answer and it's also a matter of opinion. With that in mind... I'd definitely say I tend to prefer the sound of Mahogany back and sides with a nice Sitka spruce top.
