Grand Auditorium

This guitar is designed be capable of producing the clearest and loudest tone possible. Voiced to be bold and cut through, this model is excellent for creating strong rhythm strumming patterns and clean, powerful leads.

Specifications
  • Depth at heel: 3 21/32"
  • Depth at end block: 4 5/32"
  • Shoulders: 11 3/16"
  • Waist : 9 1/8"
  • Hips: 15 1/8"
  • Sound hole: 3 7/8"
  • Body Length: 18 3/4"
All standard options available.
