Ukulele

Simple and attractive. Perfect for learning all of your favorite songs with. Available with either a Tenor scale or a Concert scale Length.

Specifications
  • Depth at heel: 1 29/32"
  • Depth at end block: 2"
  • Shoulders: 6"
  • Waist : 5 7/16"
  • Hips: 8"
  • Sound hole: 2"
  • Body Length: 11 1/2"
