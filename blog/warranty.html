Warranty

Posted on November 29, 2014

All of my instruments come with a limited lifetime warranty to the original purchaser against defects in materials and craftsmanship, for my working lifetime as a luthier. Should an instrument, at my discretion, be found defective, I will repair or cover any discussed repair costs.

Cracking of wood or finish or any other damage resulting from exposure to extremes or rapid changes of temperature or humidity, are not covered under this warranty.

Nils Guitars and Mandolins accepts no liability other than stated in the terms of this warranty.
