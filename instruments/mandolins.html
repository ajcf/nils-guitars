A-Style Mandolin

With a simple and clean cut design, this mandolin is designed to be practical. Having a flat top and a floating bridge, the top is braced to be responsive and resonate where it needs to, while still being strong enough to support the downward string tension.

Specifications
  • Depth at heel: 1 29/32"
  • Depth at end block: 2"
  • Width: 9 1/4"
  • Sound hole: 2"
  • Body Length: 12"
  • 13 78" Scale
