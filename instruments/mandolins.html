A-Style Mandolin

With a simple and clean cut design, this mandolin is designed to give players seeking a full-sounding instrument a highly functional and pleasing option. This instrument features a flat top and a floating bridge, so the top is braced to be responsive and resonate while still being strong enough to support the downward string tension.

Specifications
  • Depth at heel: 1 29/32"
  • Depth at end block: 2"
  • Width: 9 1/4"
  • Sound hole: 2"
  • Body Length: 12"

See Mandolin Ordering Page for available options.

Rosewood Back/Sides, Spruce Top, Figured Maple Neck/Binding
